Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Escape Pool Services ("we," "us") operates this website at escapepoolservices.com. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us for a quote, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

When you submit our quote form, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, ZIP code, and (optionally) email address. We also receive standard server log data — IP address, browser type, referring URL — when you visit the site.

How we use it

  • To respond to your quote request by phone, text, or email.
  • To schedule and provide pool service if you become a customer.
  • To improve the website and measure the performance of our advertising (for example, Google Ads conversion tracking).

Calls and text messages

By submitting our form or providing your phone number, you consent to receive calls and text messages from Escape Pool Services at the number you provided, including by automated means, about your quote and our services. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to any text to opt out, or HELP for help. Opting out of texts will not affect your service.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us run the business — for example, our website host (Netlify), email and SMS providers we use to reach you, and Google for ad measurement. These providers are bound to use the information only on our behalf.

Cookies and analytics

We use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure how our advertising performs. This service may set cookies in your browser to recognize repeat visits and conversions. You can opt out of Google's ad personalization at adssettings.google.com and disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your information at any time by emailing Will@escapepools.com or calling (832) 764-6224. We will respond within a reasonable time.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes
