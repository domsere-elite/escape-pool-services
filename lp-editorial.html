VI. Questions

Things homeowners ask.

How long does each weekly visit take?

Most residential pools take thirty to forty-five minutes depending on size and shade. Not in a rush, not lingering — the goal is consistent quality on a tight route.

Do you come if it's raining?

Light rain — yes, water chemistry still needs to happen. For lightning, heavy storms, or unsafe conditions we reschedule within twenty-four to forty-eight hours at no extra charge. You'll always get a heads-up text.

Do I need to be home for the visit?

No. All work happens at the pool gate. We just need access — a code, a key in a lockbox, or an unlocked side gate works. After every visit you get a service report with photos.

Are chemicals included in the price?

Yes. All standard chemicals (chlorine, stabilizer, pH and alkalinity balancers) are included. Specialty treatments — algaecides, phosphate removers, salt for chlorine generators — are quoted separately so you only pay for what your pool actually needs.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no auto-renewal traps. Most customers stay because they want to.

What if my pool is in rough shape?

If it's green or hasn't been serviced in a while, we'll quote a one-time recovery before the weekly schedule starts. Once stable, weekly service kicks in at the standard rate.
