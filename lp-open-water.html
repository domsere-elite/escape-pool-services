Common questions

What homeowners ask.

How long does each weekly visit take?

Most residential pools take 30–45 minutes depending on size and shade. We're not in a rush, but we're not lingering either — the goal is consistent quality on a tight route.

Do you come if it's raining or stormy?

Light rain — yes, water chemistry still needs to happen. For lightning, heavy storms, or unsafe conditions we reschedule within 24–48 hours at no extra charge. You'll always get a heads-up text.

Do I need to be home for the visit?

No. All work happens at the pool gate. We just need access — a code, a key in a lockbox, or an unlocked side gate works fine. After every visit you get a service report with photos.

Are chemicals included in the $199?

Yes. All standard chemicals (chlorine, stabilizer, pH and alkalinity balancers) are included. Specialty treatments — algaecides, phosphate removers, salt for chlorine generators — are quoted separately.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no auto-renewal traps. Most customers stay because they want to.

What if my pool is in rough shape?

If it's green or hasn't been serviced in a while, we'll quote a one-time recovery before the weekly schedule starts. Once stable, weekly service kicks in at the standard rate.
